Department of Electronics and Communication (E&C) Engineering:


Department of Electronics and Communication (E&C) Engineering is accredited by National Board of Accreditation (NBA), New Delhi from 01-07-2019 to 30-06-2022 for the period of 3 years.


ECE is one of the renowned branches in the field of Engineering, have proved time and again, that they play a pivotal role in both the developing and well-being of our society in the fast paced human life. E&C branch is concerned with the design, development, manufacture and application of electronic devices, circuits and systems.
